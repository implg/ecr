Dear Guests!
We invite you to the 11th annual ECR Forum.

More than 1200 delegates including representatives of ECR Russia Nonprofit Partnership company members – 60 biggest companies, leaders in FMCG and Retail industry, will take part.

The program comprises presentations dedicated to the retailers and manufacturers’ best achievements in the domain of mutual cost cuts and work with customer demand.

Our annual event will take part on the 2nd-3rd of June 2015 at the World Trade Center (Moscow, Krasnopresnenkaya nab., 12)

You will find all the information about the past events during the 10th ECR Forum in 2014 in PDF pamphlet.


Pieter Boone

Managing Director, METRO Cash & Carry Russia, ECR Russia Co-chairman

The results of 10-years work have proven the effectiveness of ECR. Consolidating retailers and suppliers, the ECR platform contributes to the exchange of best practices and the creation of common standards.

Going forward ECR will continue to further drive change and innovation covering the different areas of the ECR strategy (in particular Supply Chain and Demand Management) and optimizing other interface topics between Manufacturers and Retailers.

Maximilian Musselius

Executive Director ECR Russia

In ECR we have four major workstreams:

  1. Shopper Engagement
  2. Supply Chain Excellence
  3. Enabling Technologies
  4. People Development

The 5-th element is Trust. It is essential for efficient collaboration.

Silviu Popovici

General Manager, PepsiCo Russia, ECR Russia Co-chairman

In Russia PepsiCo is a part of several professional organizations, but I consider ECR membership as one of the most valuable. The organization brings together the suppliers and the retailers in an unique way. ECR helps them to build strong partnership in various areas: supply chain management, consumer demand, electronic data interchange, etc. For both suppliers and retailers ECR membership is a major opportunity to shape the way Russian consumers are served today and in the future. I strongly encourage the companies to join ECR.
